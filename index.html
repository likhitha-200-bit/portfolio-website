A little about me

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering undergrad who likes taking a product from a blank file to something people can actually click through. Most of my time is spent in the MERN stack, but I'm just as comfortable untangling a stubborn SQL query or arguing with a CSS flexbox at midnight.

I got into web development after building a clunky HTML page for a college club in my first year — it broke in three browsers and I couldn't stop thinking about why. A few semesters and a lot of console.log() statements later, here we are.

Outside of coursework, I'm usually solving DSA problems, reading about system design, or rebuilding this exact portfolio for what is, at this point, the fourth time.
